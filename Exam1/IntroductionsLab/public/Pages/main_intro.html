Bio 264 Lab

Welcome to BIO 264 Lab. This lab course is where we will learn a lot of basic anatomy that will serve you for years as you take higher level courses and pursue careers.

As you can see, this is an online lab text. All of the resources you will need to prepare for your exams and quizzes are linked up in this online text. Notice that the text is broken up into segments that correspond with weeks. For example, this page starts with 1.1 which means week 1 first half. 1.2 would reference week one the second half. Each week will have two parts that you will need to complete. We expect that students will divide the weeks roughly in half and complete part one the first half and part two in the second half. It generally does not work out well to save all of the assignments until the end of the week and then cram to get them done. Pay close attention to the due dates listed in the online course so that you can complete all of the graded assignments on time.

As you move through the steps listed in the menu to the left, be careful to take notes as you need to so that you can help yourself remember information, terms and concepts. Don't skip steps as they are listed in a particular order to lead you progressively through the material.

In many of the labs this semester, you will have the opportunity to use 3D models to visualize the anatomy. There will also be some models that we 3D print in lab that will help you visualize the anatomy. Anatomy is a lot of fun! We think you will enjoy learning the many important parts and pieces that make up your wonderful body.
