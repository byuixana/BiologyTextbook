Arm Muscles

This week you will learn about the muscle of the arm. You may notice that for some activities, we broke the learning up into muscles of the upper arm and muscles of the lower arm. The muscles of the upper arm are located above the elbow, although the tendons of these muscles may extend below the elbow and are of course primary elbow movers. The muscles of the lower arm are located below the elbow although the insertion of many of these muscles are located slightly above the elbow on the distil humerus. As always, it will be important to periodically return to the muscles you learn early on as you can quickly forget them as the new weeks start. Please begin by clicking on the WEEK 9.1.1.
