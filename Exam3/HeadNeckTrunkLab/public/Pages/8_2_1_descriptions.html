Learning anatomy is more than just recognizing pictures and models. It is very helpful to be able to imagine anatomical parts are in your mind with only a descripton to give you clues. This is sometimes called mental mapping of anatomy. As you study anatomy, it is a good idea to imagine in your mind how you might describe what your are looking at. You will see some questions on your quizzes and exams that ask you to identify anatomy through a small description of it. To help you practice this, we have included some cards above that have descriptions and a "show answer" button at the bottom that you can use to see if you got it right!
