3D Arm and Trunk Practice

Watch the short introductory video below and then click the small "+" icon to open the tab and reveal two options for 3D practice of arm and trunk bonea anatomy.

The first option will be titled "Clickable Arm and Trunk". This web page will open in a new page and allow you to click anywhere you want and see the name of the bone or structure you are clicking on.

The second option will be titled "Find on the Arm and Trunk". This option will open up a web page that will show a name of a random bone structure and then you can find and click it on the 3D image. If you find it, the structure will highlight. If you don't get it right, nothing will happen. You will notice that there is a button to reveal a list of structures that you can manipulate. You can choose to click or unclick structures. The program will choose random structures only from the items that are checked on the list.

It may be helpful for you to copy and save the web page links for these practice pages so that you can come back to them quickly in the future. This can provide a lot of good review and practice for you as you go over the structures.
