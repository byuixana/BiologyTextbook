3D Leg Practice

Use the small "+" icon in the menu to open the two 3D practice options for the leg muscles. You can rotate and zoom the models to review structures from multiple angles.

The Clickable Leg Muscles page lets you click structures to reveal names. The Find Leg Muscles page gives you random prompts to locate structures and check your identification skills.

Tip: bookmarking those two practice pages is useful for fast review before quizzes and exams.
